About Us

Who are we?

Started in 2020, Lebanese Services is a platform that connect employers and freelancers. Our objective is to create more opportunities for the Lebanese people to help them get through these hard times. With over 15 services offered right now, you can be guaranteed to find the adequate person for the job you need.

Lebanese Services is a startup started by Geoffry Abi Farah, Marven Abou Chehadé and Victor Hajjar with the objective of becoming leader in the online services in Lebanon and MENA region.
